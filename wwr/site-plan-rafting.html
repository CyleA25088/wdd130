What is a Site Plan?

A site plan documents purpose, audience, style, and layout decisions used to guide the site build.

Purpose

Our purpose is to offer exciting and safe white water rafting trips for everyone, from beginners to experienced rafters. We want to help people enjoy nature, have fun, and learn about taking care of our rivers.

Audience

The audience for my website would be anyone looking for some fun, adventure, or just an activity to do during their free time.

Personas and Scenarios

  • Outdoor Families: Looking for safe half-day rafting trips, mobile-first browsing.
  • Adventure Seekers: Want multi-day rapids and camping + booking, likely desktop/tablet.
