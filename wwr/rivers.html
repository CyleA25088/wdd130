Explore Our Rivers

Find the perfect adventure for your thrill level and schedule.

Snake River

Difficulty: Beginner–Intermediate

Perfect for families and first-time rafters. Gentle rapids wind through scenic canyons, offering wildlife sightings and swimming spots along the way.

Colorado River

Difficulty: Intermediate–Advanced

Our most popular run! Navigate Class III–IV rapids through breathtaking red-rock gorges. Multi-day trips include riverside camping and stargazing.

Salmon River

Difficulty: Advanced

Nicknamed “The River of No Return,” this trip is for seasoned rafters seeking adrenaline-packed waves and unforgettable mountain vistas.

Verde River

Difficulty: Beginner

Short but exciting day trips ideal for families or corporate outings. Calm stretches with fun splashes and lots of opportunities for pictures!
